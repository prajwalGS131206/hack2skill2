How the Decision Engine Works

    

      

        
🚨

        
Emergency Priority

        
High-urgency users are always routed to the fastest available option — including mode switches.

      

      

        
🔄

        
Reroute Logic

        
Stuck users in high/extreme traffic are rerouted via alternate paths to clear congestion zones.

      

      

        
🚲

        
Mode Switching

        
Promotes lighter transport (bike, metro, walk) to reduce density and improve system-wide flow.

      

      

        
⏳

        
Delay Recommendation

        
Low-urgency users in extreme traffic are advised to delay, easing peak-hour load.

      

      

        
🧮

        
Weighted Scoring

        
Each action is scored on a multi-factor matrix: traffic density, urgency, mode efficiency & user type.

      

      

        
📊

        
Confidence Scoring

        
Every decision comes with a confidence % showing how strongly the AI preferred that action over alternatives.
